Suitable database schema for this application--

Table1---User:
Columns:
user_id (Primary Key)
name
email
mobile_number


Table2--Spend:
expense_id (Primary Key)
payer_id (References from table User)
amount
expense_type

Table3--Splits:
split_id (Primary Key)
expense_id (references from Spend)
user_id (references from User)
amount
